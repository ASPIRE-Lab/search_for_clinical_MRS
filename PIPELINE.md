## I. Overview
**Task**: Generate a list of the most influential clinical journals from each specialty that publish MRS research. 

* Identification of clinical specialties 
* Generate a list of the top-cited biomedical journals with categories that spanned across clinical specialties using Journal Citation Reports (JCR)
	* Decide on filter and use to limit search results 
* Identify the relevant in vivo MRS papers published (and publishers) 

## II. Background 


#### Clinical Specialties
Identified using CUMC and supplemented by clinical categories on JCR/WOS
[vagelos.columbia.edu/departments-centers/clinical-departments-and-divisions](https://www.vagelos.columbia.edu/departments-centers/clinical-departments-and-divisions)


#### Journal Citation Reports
[Journal Citation Reports (JCR)](https://clarivate.com/webofsciencegroup/solutions/journal-citation-reports/) contains 27,920 journals for all years  (21,494 journals for 2021). 

Primarily filtered by journal category, i.e. out of 254 available categories, 43 categories were manually selected because they corresponded with clinical medicine specialties. Then filtered further by way of Journal Impact Factor (JIF) filter, specifically a JIF ≥ 2.

> Level 0 (no filter): 21,494 journals*
>  
> Level 1 (category filter): 4,321 journals*
>  
> Level 2 (JIF filter): 2,964 journals*

*Note, all numbers and values are accurate as of 10/31/22, the date of the original search. A previous iteration of the search conducted on of 7/27/2022, yielded 2,362 journals which met these criteria.


#### Requirements
Access to [Web of Science (WOS)](https://www.webofscience.com/wos/woscc/advanced-search), a publisher-neutral electronic databases by Clarivate
